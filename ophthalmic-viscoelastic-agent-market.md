<p><p>The ophthalmic viscoelastic agent (OBA) market features two primary types: cohesive viscoelastic and low viscosity dispersion viscoelastic. Cohesive viscoelastic agents offer superior stability and are ideal for maintaining space during surgical procedures, such as cataract surgery. In contrast, low viscosity dispersion viscoelastic agents are designed for easier injection and smoother tissue interaction, making them suitable for procedures requiring less resistance. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing surgical outcomes and protecting ocular tissues during operations.</p></p>
