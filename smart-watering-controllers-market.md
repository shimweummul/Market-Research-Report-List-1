<p><p>Smart watering controllers enhance irrigation efficiency by adapting to environmental conditions. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time meteorological data, adjusting watering schedules based on forecasts, rainfall, and temperature, thus conserving water and preventing overwatering. Conversely, soil moisture sensor-based controllers monitor moisture levels in the soil, activating irrigation only when needed, ensuring optimal hydration for plants. Both technologies aim to promote sustainable water usage in landscaping and agriculture while improving plant health and reducing water waste.</p></p>
