<p><p>The automotive window sealing systems market features key material types: EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomers), and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride). EPDM is favored for its excellent weather resistance and durability, making it ideal for long-lasting seals. TPE offers versatility and ease of processing, providing flexibility in design and application. PVC is valued for its cost-effectiveness and good sealing properties, though it may be less resilient to environmental factors. Each material serves distinct needs in automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
