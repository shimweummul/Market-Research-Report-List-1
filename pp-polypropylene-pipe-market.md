<p><strong>The PP (Polypropylene) Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PP-R/RCT Pipe</li><li>PP-H Pipe</li><li>PP-B Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene (PP) pipe market encompasses three main types: PP-R/RCT, PP-H, and PP-B. PP-R (Random Copolymer) and RCT (Random Copolymer with improved stiffness) pipes are known for their excellent temperature resistance, making them ideal for hot and cold water systems. PP-H (Homopolymer) pipes offer high chemical resistance and are commonly used in industrial applications. PP-B (Block Copolymer) pipes combine strength with flexibility, suitable for various plumbing and drainage systems, providing versatility across multiple industries.</p></p>
