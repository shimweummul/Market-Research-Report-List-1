<p><strong>The Lined Plug Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PFA Lined</li><li>PTFE Lined</li><li>FEP Lined</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lined plug valves are designed for handling corrosive substances in various industries. The PFA lined type features a perfluoroalkoxy fluoropolymer coating, providing excellent chemical resistance and thermal stability. PTFE lined valves utilize polytetrafluoroethylene for similar benefits, ensuring low friction and high durability. FEP lined valves, made with fluorinated ethylene propylene, offer transparency and good chemical resistance, suitable for applications requiring visibility of flow. The "Others" category includes alternative linings like ECTFE or rubber, addressing specific process needs.</p></p>
