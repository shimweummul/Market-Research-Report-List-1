<p><p>The melamine foam market is categorized into three types: rigid, semi-rigid, and flexible melamine foam. Rigid melamine foam is characterized by its hard structure, ideal for thermal insulation and soundproofing applications. Semi-rigid melamine foam offers a balance between softness and rigidity, making it suitable for various cushioning and packaging needs. Flexible melamine foam is highly adaptable, providing excellent acoustic and thermal insulation while allowing for easy manipulation in diverse applications, including automotive and building materials.</p></p>
