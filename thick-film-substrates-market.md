<p><p>The thick film substrates market is categorized into single-layer and multilayer types. Single-layer thick film substrates consist of a single insulative layer coated with thick conductive materials, ideal for straightforward applications requiring high reliability. In contrast, multilayer thick film substrates feature multiple conductive and dielectric layers, enabling more complex circuit designs and integrated functions. This multilayer approach enhances device miniaturization and performance, making it suitable for advanced electronics in telecommunications, automotive, and consumer products.</p></p>
