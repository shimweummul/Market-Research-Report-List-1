<p><p>A Vertical Lift Module (VLM) is an automated storage and retrieval system designed to optimize warehouse and inventory management by utilizing vertical space. These systems consist of two parallel columns of trays that ascend and descend to deliver items efficiently to an ergonomic pick window.
