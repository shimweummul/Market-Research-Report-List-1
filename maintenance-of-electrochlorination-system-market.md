<p><p>The electrochlorination system market features two main types of maintenance: brine and seawater systems. Brine system maintenance involves managing the operation and upkeep of systems that convert saltwater into chlorine, primarily using a concentrated salt solution. This process requires regular checks on electrodes and cell performance to ensure efficient chlorine production. In contrast, seawater system maintenance focuses on systems utilizing natural seawater for chlorination, emphasizing the monitoring of water quality and system integrity to optimize disinfection processes.</p></p>
