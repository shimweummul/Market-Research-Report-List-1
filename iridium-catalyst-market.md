<p><p>The Iridium Catalyst market is primarily segmented into two types: particle and powder. Particle catalysts consist of larger, discrete grains that offer high surface area for reactions, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, powder catalysts feature finer particles that enhance dispersion and reactivity, often used in smaller-scale or specialized processes. Both forms are essential in various chemical reactions, including fuel cells and petrochemical processes, influencing efficiency and performance in catalyst applications.</p></p>
