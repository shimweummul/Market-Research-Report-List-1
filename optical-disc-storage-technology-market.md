<p><ul><li>Read Only</li><li>Write-once</li><li>Erasable Rewritable</li><li>Photo Disc</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical disc storage technology encompasses several market types based on data write capabilities. Read-only discs, like CDs and DVDs, are pre-recorded and can't be modified. Write-once discs allow data to be written once and then used similarly to read-only discs. Erasable rewritable discs enable users to write, erase, and rewrite data multiple times. Photo discs are specialized optical media optimized for storing high-quality images, providing enhanced durability and compatibility for photographers and digital artists. Each type serves distinct data storage needs.</p></p>
