<p><p>The cardiovascular drug market encompasses various types of medications used to manage heart conditions and improve cardiovascular health. Heparin is an anticoagulant that prevents blood clots, while Coumadin (warfarin) is an oral anticoagulant used for similar purposes. Zebeta (bisoprolol) and Lopressor (metoprolol) are beta-blockers that help manage hypertension and heart rhythm disorders. Other medications in this market include ACE inhibitors, statins, and antiarrhythmics, which all contribute to the treatment of cardiovascular diseases and the overall improvement of heart health.</p></p>
