<p><p>The Chip-On-Film (COF) market is divided into two main types: Single Layer and Double Layer. Single Layer COF involves attaching a chip to a flexible film layer, providing a compact solution ideal for thinner devices. In contrast, Double Layer COF incorporates two layers of film, allowing for enhanced performance and increased connectivity options. This dual-layer approach supports complex applications in advanced electronics, making it suitable for high-resolution displays and intricate circuit designs, catering to diverse market demands.</p></p>
