<p><p>The melamine foam sponge market is categorized into three types: rigid, semi-rigid, and flexible melamine foam. Rigid melamine foam is known for its excellent sound absorption and insulation properties, making it ideal for construction and automotive applications. Semi-rigid melamine foam offers a balance between firmness and flexibility, suitable for a variety of uses, including thermal insulation. Flexible melamine foam is soft and pliable, often used in padded applications like upholstery and packaging, providing comfort and protection.</p></p>
