<p><strong>The Chemical Lidar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Range Lidar</li><li>Midrange Lidar</li><li>Long-range Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemical Lidar market is categorized into three types based on operational range: short-range, midrange, and long-range Lidar. Short-range Lidar is designed for detecting and analyzing chemical compounds over distances of up to a few kilometers, primarily used in urban environments. Midrange Lidar operates effectively between a few kilometers to tens of kilometers, suitable for environmental monitoring. Long-range Lidar encompasses distances beyond tens of kilometers, allowing for extensive surveillance and detection of atmospheric pollutants at significant ranges.</p></p>
