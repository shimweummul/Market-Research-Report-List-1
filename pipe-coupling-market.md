<p><p>The pipe coupling market is divided into two main types: metal and plastic couplings. Metal couplings are typically made from materials such as stainless steel or brass, offering high durability and strength, making them ideal for high-pressure applications in industries like oil and gas. On the other hand, plastic couplings, often made from PVC or polypropylene, are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and suitable for low-pressure applications, making them popular in construction and agricultural sectors. Both types serve essential roles in fluid transfer systems.</p></p>
