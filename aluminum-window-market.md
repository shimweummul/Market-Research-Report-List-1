<p><ul><li>Fix Sash</li><li>Opening Sash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum window market is divided into two primary types: fixed sash and opening sash windows. Fixed sash windows are non-operable and designed primarily for aesthetics and allowing light into a space, providing a seamless view without ventilation. In contrast, opening sash windows can be operated to allow airflow, enhancing indoor comfort. These include designs like casement, sliding, and double-hung windows. Both types are popular for their durability, low maintenance, and energy efficiency in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
