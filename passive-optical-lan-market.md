<p><strong>The Passive Optical LAN Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GPON</li><li>EPON</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Passive Optical LAN (POL) market primarily consists of two technologies: GPON (Gigabit Passive Optical Network) and EPON (Ethernet Passive Optical Network). GPON leverages time-division multiplexing to deliver high-speed internet, voice, and video services, making it suitable for residential and enterprise applications. In contrast, EPON utilizes Ethernet technology for data transmission, offering cost-effective solutions for broadband access, particularly in urban environments. Both types facilitate efficient fiber-optic connectivity, supporting a range of applications across various sectors.</p></p>
