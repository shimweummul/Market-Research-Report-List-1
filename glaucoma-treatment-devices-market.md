<p><p>The glaucoma treatment devices market encompasses various types designed to manage intraocular pressure in glaucoma patients. Glaucoma drainage devices include implants that help facilitate fluid drainage from the eye, reducing pressure. Both types are essential in modern glaucoma management, offering less invasive options for effective treatment.</p></p>
