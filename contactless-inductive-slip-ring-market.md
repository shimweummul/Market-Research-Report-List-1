<p><p>The contactless inductive slip ring market is categorized into four main types: electric, hydraulic, hybrid, and pneumatic. Electric slip rings use electromagnetic induction for seamless power and data transmission, ideal for applications requiring continuous rotation. Hydraulic slip rings, employing fluid power, are suited for high torque demands. Hybrid slip rings combine electric and hydraulic functions, offering versatility across various systems. Pneumatic slip rings utilize air pressure for motion control, commonly found in automated equipment and robotics, enhancing performance in dynamic environments.</p></p>
