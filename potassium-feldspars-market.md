<p><p>The potassium feldspar market is segmented into three main types: glass grade, ceramic body grade, and glaze grade. Glass grade potassium feldspar is utilized for its high purity and low iron content, enhancing glass clarity and strength. Ceramic body grade is used in the production of ceramics, contributing to durability and aesthetic qualities. Glaze grade potassium feldspar serves as a flux in glazes, ensuring proper melting and achieving desired surface finishes in ceramic applications. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
