<p><p>The heat pump dehumidifier market is primarily categorized into two types: tower type and rotor type. Tower type dehumidifiers use a vertical design that allows for efficient airflow and moisture removal, making them suitable for larger spaces. In contrast, rotor type dehumidifiers utilize a rotating wheel to enhance the absorption of moisture, offering improved energy efficiency and compactness, ideal for smaller environments. Both types serve to regulate humidity levels, enhancing comfort and preserving materials in various applications.</p></p>
