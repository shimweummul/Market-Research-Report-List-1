<p><p>Mineral Insulated Resistance Thermometers (RTDs) are temperature sensors that use a metal wire conductor insulated with mineral material, typically magnesium oxide, to provide accurate temperature readings in harsh environments. The market for RTDs is categorized into simplex and duplex conductor types. Simplex conductors contain one sensing element, ideal for basic applications, while duplex conductors feature two sensing elements, allowing for redundancy and increased accuracy. These types are essential in various industrial applications, including manufacturing, oil and gas, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
