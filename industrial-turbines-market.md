<p><p>The industrial turbines market is primarily divided into gas turbines and steam turbines. Gas turbines are known for their efficiency and versatility, commonly used in power generation and jet propulsion. They operate on the Brayton cycle, utilizing compressed air and fuel combustion for energy conversion. In contrast, steam turbines harness thermal energy from steam produced by boiling water, often used in power plants and industrial applications. Together, these technologies significantly contribute to energy production and industrial processes, catering to diverse energy needs.</p></p>
