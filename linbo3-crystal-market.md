<p><p>The LiNbO3 crystal market is segmented into two primary types: acoustic grade and optical grade. Acoustic grade LiNbO3 is mainly used in applications involving ultrasonic devices and surface acoustic wave (SAW) technology, where its piezoelectric properties are crucial. Conversely, optical grade LiNbO3 is essential for photonic applications, such as wavelength converters, modulators, and second harmonic generation, due to its excellent nonlinear optical characteristics. Both grades cater to distinct industrial needs, driving their respective market demand.</p></p>
