<p><strong>The Audio Amplifier ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio amplifier ICs market comprises primarily two types: surface mount and through-hole. Surface mount technology (SMT) allows for compact design and efficient assembly, making it ideal for modern electronics where space is a concern. Conversely, through-hole components are mounted by inserting leads into circuit boards, offering robustness and easier repair, particularly in DIY and industrial applications. The choice between these types often depends on design requirements, manufacturing processes, and cost considerations within audio amplification systems.</p></p>
