<p><p>The hydraulic seals market comprises three main types: hydraulic piston seals, hydraulic rod seals, and hydraulic wiper seals. Hydraulic piston seals are designed to prevent fluid leakage between the piston and cylinder, ensuring efficient operation. Hydraulic rod seals seal the gap between the rod and the cylinder, preventing fluid loss while allowing smooth rod movement. Hydraulic wiper seals are positioned at the cylinder's entry point, removing dirt and debris to protect internal components, enhancing system longevity and performance.</p></p>
