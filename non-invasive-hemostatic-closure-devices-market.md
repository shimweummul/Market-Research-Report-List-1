<p><p>The Non-Invasive Hemostatic Closure Devices market encompasses various types including closure strips, tissue adhesives, and sutures. Closure strips are adhesive strips applied to wounds to promote skin adhesion and enhance healing. Tissue adhesives are medical glues that bond skin edges together, offering a quick and effective closure method. Sutures involve stitching tissues together using threads, providing secure closure but often requiring a more invasive procedure. Each type serves to control bleeding and facilitate wound healing in minimally invasive settings.</p></p>
