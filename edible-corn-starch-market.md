<p><strong>The Edible Corn Starch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amylose Corn Starch</li><li>Amylopectin Corn Starch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The edible corn starch market is primarily divided into two types: amylose corn starch and amylopectin corn starch. Amylose corn starch, known for its linear structure, provides a gel-like texture and is often used in food applications requiring thickening and stabilizing properties. In contrast, amylopectin corn starch has a branched structure, resulting in higher viscosity and a smoother texture, making it suitable for products like sauces and gravies. Both types play crucial roles in food formulation and texture enhancement.</p></p>
