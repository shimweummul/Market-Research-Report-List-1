<p><p>The LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) and MPI (Modified Polyimide) Flexible Copper Clad Laminate (FCCL) markets cater to advanced electronic applications, offering distinct characteristics. LCP FCCL is known for its high thermal stability, low dielectric loss, and excellent chemical resistance, making it ideal for high-frequency and high-performance devices. In contrast, MPI FCCL provides superior flexibility, thermal endurance, and mechanical strength, suitable for various flexible and rigid-flex circuits. Both materials are pivotal in technologies like smartphones, wearables, and automotive electronics.</p></p>
