<p><ul><li>Gas Delivery Pump</li><li>Gas Adsorption Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lab vacuum pump market comprises various types, primarily focusing on Gas Delivery Pumps and Gas Adsorption Pumps. Gas Delivery Pumps are designed to create vacuum conditions by removing gases efficiently, facilitating processes such as evaporation and distillation. In contrast, Gas Adsorption Pumps utilize adsorbent materials to capture and retain gas molecules, effectively establishing a vacuum. Both types are essential in laboratory settings for various applications, including research, development, and industrial processes, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency in experiments.</p></p>
