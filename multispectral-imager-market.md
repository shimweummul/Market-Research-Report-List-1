<p><strong>The Multispectral Imager Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-lens Type</li><li>Multi-camera Type</li><li>Beam Split Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multispectral imager market comprises three primary types: Multi-lens Type, Multi-camera Type, and Beam Split Type. The Multi-lens Type utilizes multiple lenses to capture various spectral bands simultaneously, optimizing spatial resolution. The Multi-camera Type involves multiple cameras, each tuned to different wavelengths, allowing for high spectral fidelity but requiring more space. The Beam Split Type employs a beam splitter to separate light into different wavelengths within a single camera, balancing complexity and size while maintaining image quality.</p></p>
