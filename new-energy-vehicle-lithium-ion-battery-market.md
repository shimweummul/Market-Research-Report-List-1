<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle lithium-ion battery market consists of various battery types, each with unique properties. Lithium Manganese Oxide (LMO) provides high thermal stability and energy density, suitable for power applications. Lithium Cobaltate (LCO) delivers excellent energy density but has lower thermal stability. Other types include Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt (NMC), which balances performance and cost, and can cater to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
