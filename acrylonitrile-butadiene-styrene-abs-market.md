<p><p>Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) is a versatile thermoplastic used in various applications due to its excellent mechanical properties and impact resistance. The ABS market is segmented into different types based on the polymerization methods. Emulsion graft copolymerization involves dispersing acrylonitrile and styrene onto butadiene latex, enhancing compatibility and performance. Bulk polymerization is a more straightforward method, combining all components in a single phase for efficiency. Other methods include suspension and solution polymerization, each offering unique characteristics tailored to specific industrial applications.</p></p>
