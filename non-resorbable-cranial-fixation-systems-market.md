<p><p>Non-resorbable cranial fixation systems are medical devices used during neurosurgical procedures to secure and stabilize cranial bone fragments after surgeries such as craniotomy or traumatic brain injuries. Their primary application is in cranial reconstruction and repair, ensuring proper alignment and stabilization of skull components.</p><p>The Non-resorbable Cranial Fixation Systems Market is expected to experience significant growth, with a projected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 14.3% during the forecast period.
